Experience gaming freedom with free, light, and fun rules, empowering you to dive into epic battles without restrictions. Cadence is a modular, open-licensed system that sets the stage for a thriving community of game enthusiasts, where you can collaborate, share ideas, and shape the future of tabletop gaming together.

Unleash your creativity and tap into a world of limitless possibilities. With this versatile game engine, you have the power to bring your unique vision to life. Whether you're seeking personal enjoyment or exploring new horizons in the gaming industry, the Cadence System empowers you to craft captivating games and embark on an unforgettable journey.

The core rules are used in every Cadence Game Module and are quick and easy to learn, while offering enough detail for a dynamic and exciting game. It features alternating activations, easy to learn actions, and familiar d6 based mechanics. PDF and Quick Start rules coming soon.
